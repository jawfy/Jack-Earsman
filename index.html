About Me


Insperation

My brother is a designer & website developer which is where I got the inspiration... I was 9 when I started to bug my brother, I'd come sit in his bedroom constantly asking questions and watching what he was doing. I believe that that day is what first sparked design in my head, I can remember one day I came into his room and he was on photoshop cs5 designing a communication type icon, it had two ellipse objects with a little arrow at the bottom right-hand side of each, he spent hours if not days perfecting the design to eventually see it appear in the footer of his and his partners app called; interstateapp.


Learning

This amazed me to see something come from what was built by my brother to appear on an interactive website! It attracted me to design rather than HTML but I always wanted to explore it yet it took me 3 years from 9 to finally try website development. In that 3 years, I focused on photoshop and design, I spent hours each day messing around with tools, watching online tutorials and reading articles to eventually get to grips with how design and photoshop worked.


Website Development

12, almost knowing everything about photoshop and what a good design looks like I took to website development. I took the same approach that I did with design only I would download templates and mess around with the code rather than tools. It took about a year of this when I felt confident with HTML and CSS.


Present Day

I am now a designer born and brought up in Scotland. I'm now 14 years of age teaching myself new things every day. I'm now looking forward to continuing my career onto University in a couple of years to eventually get a job.
